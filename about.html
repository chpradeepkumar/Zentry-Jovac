Company & Product Story

Zentry began in 2025 during an intensive open-source systems hackathon. The founders noticed engineering units spend a disproportionate amount of production time simply maintaining system coordination scripts. We built Zentry to abstract background processes away completely.

Product Vision

Our long-term product vision is to deliver clean, completely serverless workflow automation loops that scale organically alongside team growth with absolute minimal upkeep costs.
